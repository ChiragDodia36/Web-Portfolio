Futuristic macOS Portfolio Concepts

3 takes on a macOS-inspired 3D portfolio. Each is animated below.

A
macOS Desktop Experience RECOMMENDED
Your portfolio IS a futuristic macOS desktop. A dock at the bottom, floating windows for each section, a dynamic wallpaper behind everything. Click dock icons to open "apps" — About.app, Projects.app, Skills.app. Windows can be dragged, minimized, layered.
B
Vision Pro Spatial UI
Inspired by Apple Vision Pro — floating translucent panels in a dark 3D void. As you scroll, panels glide toward you with depth-of-field blur. Each section is a spatial card hovering in space with glass morphism and subtle light refractions.
C
Finder Journey
A cinematic Finder-like file browser in 3D space. Your career is organized as folders and files that open as you scroll — each "folder" expands into a 3D scene. Column view navigation, but the columns stretch into infinite depth. Scroll = navigating deeper into your story.
Click "Select" on your favorite, then return to the terminal.
